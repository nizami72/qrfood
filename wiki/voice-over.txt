1 Registration and Restaurant Setup
Getting started is fast. Fill out the registration form that consists of name, email address, password and restaurant name to create your account; after successful completion, you will be redirected to log in form. Filling it out brings you to the Restaurant page where you can click 'Edit Restaurant' to add additional information. Fill in the details and save. Your restaurant is now live.
2 Menu Creation
Next, build your menu. Quickly add categories, either manually or from our predefined list. Then, populate them with your dishes, complete with multilingual names if you would like to have English and Russian profile as well, descriptions, and prices.
3 Tables and QR Codes
Now, let's set up your tables. Just add a table number and it's ready. A unique QR code is instantly generated for each table. Simply print it, place it on the table, and you're ready to offer a seamless, contactless ordering experience.
4 Receiving Orders
When a customer scans the QR code and places an order, it appears instantly on your live dashboard, highlighted with a fire icon for immediate attention. You'll see every new order in real-time, keeping your kitchen perfectly in sync with your customers.
5 Quick Order Control
Managing your workflow is effortless. With a single click, you can update an order's status from 'Preparing' to 'Ready,' ensuring a smooth and efficient service from kitchen to table. Take control of your orders with ease.

0-25.7 Register and login

Giriş etdikdən sonra sizi "Restoran" səhifəsi qarşılayacaq. Burada "Restoranı redaktə et" düyməsinə klikləyərək əlavə məlumatlar əlavə edə bilərsiniz. Məlumatları doldurun və yadda saxlayın. Artıq restoranınız aktivdir.

25.7-42.2 Create menu

42.2 - 48.1 Create table
3 İndi isə masalarınızı qurmağa başlayaq. Sadəcə masa nömrəsini, istəsəniz oturacaq sayı və qeyd əlavə edin – və hər şey hazırdır. Hər masa üçün unikal QR kod avtomatik yaradılır. Onu çap edin, masanın üzərinə yerləşdirin və artıq təmassız sifariş sisteminiz istifadəyə hazırdır.

48.1-65 Scan QR code and receive orders

65-79 Manage order
